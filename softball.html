I normally played in the infeild and sometimes very rarely I played outfeild. The main bases I was good at were 1st, 3rd and shotstop, when I played softball I have been injuried quite a bit. I almost broke my right ankle but never had it looked at so it never got heeled right.

Softball can be a very compicanted game and someone can get hurt very easly, I remember always having buires all over from getting hit by a softball. I'm very forchenit that I actually never broke nothing but I have been hurt very bad before.
